Ancient wisdom and modern science of prenatal development:

Building a new healthy world from the womb

Objective
The main objective in organizing this conference is to explore the ancient wisdom as also to establish the scientific basis of Garbha Sanskar for the benefit of all.

Who can participate?

Psychologists, Psychiatrists, Gynecologists, Obstetricians, Neurologists, Geneticists, Pediatricians, Nurses, Midwives, Doulas, Institutes/Universities/ Research Centers conducting/organizing Garbha Sanskar, Pregnant couples, Students and anyone interested in the Birthing Process.

Speakers

Scientists, researchers, scholars, doctors of world repute from both fields - ancient wisdom and modern science - will be enlightening the august audience, in this conference.
