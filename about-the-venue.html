Manashakti Research Centre

Non-Profit NGO

Manashakti Research Centre is a non-profit NGO located in the pleasant Sahyadri hills at Lonavla, INDIA.

Research Based

Research is conducted right from the pre-conception and pre-natal state up to life-after-death stages.

Scientific Means

The centre strives to help people achieve tension-free success and peace of mind through scientific means and social welfare activities.

For past 50+ years, millions of people have found solace through following means:

  • Seminars
  • Social activities
  • Electronic, computerized MIND tests
  • Workshops
  • Literature
  • Ayurveda products
  • Magazines
  • Study courses
  • Experimental aids
